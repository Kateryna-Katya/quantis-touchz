Procedure for Collection, Storage, and Destruction of Data

We collect personal data exclusively to provide high-quality access to technology: participation in intensives, receiving guides, and personal consultations. To prevent leaks, a set of information security measures compliant with EU standards is used.

The maximum data retention period is 75 years (for archival records and legal security); however, in most cases, data is stored until the termination of the platform's activities or the withdrawal of consent by the user.

The destruction or anonymization of data is performed to exclude the possibility of further processing once the purpose of collection has been achieved or upon the user's request.

Data Transfer to Third Parties:

Quantis-Touchz does not provide user data to third parties, except for:

  • The presence of your direct consent;
