Cookie Policy

Welcome to Quantis-Touchz! To enhance your experience with our platform dedicated to AI practical applications, ensure its proper functioning, and analyze user activity within EU countries, we use cookies and similar technologies. This policy explains in detail which technologies we use, for what purposes, and how you can manage them.

What are cookies?

A cookie is a small piece of data (text file) that a website stores on your device when you visit it. This allows Quantis-Touchz to "remember" your actions and preferences (such as interface language, access region, or AI content display settings) over a period of time.

What types of cookies do we use?

We classify the cookies used on the Quantis-Touchz website into the following categories:

  • Strictly Necessary: Critical for the platform's operation. They allow you to navigate through the "Innovations" and "Practices" sections and use basic features.
  • Analytical and Performance: Help us understand which AI tools are most interesting to our audience in France and other European countries. All data is collected anonymously.
  • Functional: Allow the site to remember your choices to provide a more personalized AI learning experience.
  • Marketing: Used to show relevant updates about new AI solutions that might be useful specifically to you.

Why do we use cookies?

The main purposes of using cookies on the Quantis-Touchz website are:

  • Ensuring stable operation of content filtering algorithms.
  • Behavioral analysis to optimize the structure of educational blocks.
  • Adapting the platform to EU digital infrastructure standards.

Your choice and cookie management

You have full control over cookies. You can change your browser settings or withdraw your consent through our Cookie popup at any time. Please note: disabling strictly necessary cookies may limit access to interactive elements of our AI practices.

Policy Updates

We may update this Policy from time to time to reflect changes in AI technology or French and EU legislation. Continued use of our site after changes are made will constitute your consent to them.
